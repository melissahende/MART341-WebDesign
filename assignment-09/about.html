Why did I choose this project?

There are many different golf clubs to choose from when golfing. Many people may not know the difference between the different kinds of clubs and their names.




What advice do I have for beginners?

Patience is key! Golf is a lot more difficult than you may think. Work on making solid contact with the ball and getting comfortable swinging a club. Then you can get technical with your technique
