Roasted Asparagus

Ingredients

  • Asparagus
  • Half stick of butter
  • Garlic salt to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425oF.
  2. Spread asparagus over baking sheet evenly.
  3. Sprinkle garlic salt to taste over the asparagus.
  4. Cut butter into cubes and place on top of the aspargus evenly distributed.
  5. Place in oven and cook until desired tenderness.
